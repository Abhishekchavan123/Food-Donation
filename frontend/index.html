About Us

DonateEasy is an innovative platform designed to bridge the gap between food donors and NGOs. Our mission is simple yet powerful: reduce food waste and feed the hungry. Using smart technology, we connect surplus food from restaurants, hotels, events, and individuals to verified NGOs and volunteers for timely distribution.

From real-time food availability updates to seamless pickup scheduling, we ensure that every extra meal finds its way to someone in need. Together, we can create a world where no plate is wasted, and no person sleeps hungry.
